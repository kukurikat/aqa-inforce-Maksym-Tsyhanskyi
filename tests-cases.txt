1. Check that the room can be booked with valid data

Steps
1. Go to https://automationintesting.online/
2. Click on Rooms button on the NavBar
3. Choose any room and press Book now
4. Choose any dates from today by holding and dragging on the calendar 
5. Press Reserve Now 
6. Fill the fields with valid information
7. Press Reserve Now 

Expected result
Booking Confirmed text is displayed. Correct date range is shown.

2. Check that the room can’t be booked with invalid data

Steps
1. Go to https://automationintesting.online/
2. Click on Rooms button on the NavBar
3. Choose any room and press Book now
4. Choose any dates from today by holding and dragging on the calendar 
5. Press Reserve Now 
6. Fill the email fields with invalid data (e.g. One letter name, letters in phone number field)
7. Press Reserve Now

Expected result
Error message displayed under the info fields. Booking is not confirmed.

3. Check that the earlier booked dates show as Unavailable
Steps
1. Go to https://automationintesting.online/
2. Click on Rooms button on the NavBar
3. Choose any room and book it for valid dates
4. Go to Main page
5. Choose Check In Date one day behind the booked room period
6. Choose Check In Date one day after the booked room period
7. Press Check Availability

Expected result
Booked room is not shown as room available to book.
